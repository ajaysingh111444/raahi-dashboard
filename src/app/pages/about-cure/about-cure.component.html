We Are

Transforming our health system and making medical care available for every citizen without any burden of extra cost. As it is the basic need not luxury, so everyone should have access to proper medical care.

RAAHI is an initiative taken by "Humanity Welfare Council" where we aim to transform the health care sector globally by providing necessary funds to people in urgent need of medical assistance and bringing advancement in the medical system with increased accessibility.

Humanity Welfare Council is a social welfare organisation committed to creating a developed and healthy world. And in the race of development the health sector tops the list.

A healthy human is a resource for the nation. If a person is physically and mentally fit then only he can think beyond his zone and work for the upliftment of the nation. Therefore a healthy body is a temple where a healthy mind resides.

We are focusing on creating a healthy environment for the progressive approach towards the developing world. We want free medical aid for every individual and build Disease-free human race. We are creating a wider platform where everyone can easily reach in case of medical emergency without any unnecessary formalities and delayed time in attending to their medical concerns. At this platform, we will also unite medical institutions and government and non-government bodies under one roof to find solutions to globally arising medical concerns.

We are working on creating high medical solutions at affordable cost by combining Ayurveda and allopathy.

We are transparent regarding our daily fund operations and assistance services. We hold ourselves liable to our trusted donors and people who approach us for help.
