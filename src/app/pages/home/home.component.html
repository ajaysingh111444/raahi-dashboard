Why Us?

Medical Emergencies can be devastating and can turn the life of an individual and their loved ones upside down. The hardest part of life is to choose between coping with life's needs or medical urgency. No one asks for sickness, it just knocks at our door like an uninvited guest. With the changing of the world at a fast pace, drastic climate changes are leading to new medical emergencies as there is a lot to be uncovered on this planet. And such medical emergencies are like alarming situations in our lives.

We Are

Transforming our health system and making medical care available for every citizen without the burden of extra cost. As it is a basic need, not a luxury, so everyone should have access to proper medical care.

RAAHI is an initiative taken by the "Humanity Welfare Council" where we aim to transform the health care sector globally by providing necessary funds to people in urgent need of medical assistance and bringing advancement in the medical system with increased accessibility.
