The Team

Surya Pratap Singh

Project Director

A person who adds a new definition to anything he touches. Some people know him as an Entrepreneur; some know him as a poet, for some he is a game changer, for others he is a man of hope. A man who keeps his profile low as according to him "I am just doing my work", Surya Pratap Singh is the founder of Humanity Welfare Council and has set up many business verticals. The main reason to admire him is that he donates whatever he earns. After working a lot and with many organizations he somewhere realized that the condition of his country can't become better unless a very transparent organization is formed with a hard working and dedicated team. He then took a decision to form an organization on which people can rely and which will deliver results on ground level and not just paper work. So, Humanity Welfare Council came into existence on 8th Nov 2017. After a lot of hard work and a lot of research, projects were designed. These projects were based on the needs of the common people, needs which if unfulfilled were the biggest obstruction in the development of the nation.

Udita Saikia

Assistant Director

Udita is a young and enthusiastic social worker who genuinely cares for the society and wants to make it better in every possible way. She holds a Masters degree in Social Work from Amity University and has gained valuable work experience while working for major welfare organizations in different parts of the country. A positive approach towards every situation and she connects well with people from diverse backgrounds and age groups. She builds a bond of trust and wins hearts with her dedication to work. She Says, “ Since I was a child, Helping others has given me immense satisfaction. The caring attitude and my compassionate nature certainly inspired me to choose this career as a social worker.” Her Motto - A change starts from you so, be the change you wish to see in the world.
