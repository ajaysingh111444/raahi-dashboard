What We Do

RAAHI is currently serving the people of India with its wings spread in 28+ states & UT in India. Our team at RAAHI is currently raising funds all over India for supporting people during medical emergencies and the one who loose their sole earning member of the family. We are taking initiatives towards technological advancements in the field of medical assistance and making it accessible for all.

We are Committed towards:

  • Eliminating pain and suffering from the world.
  • To provide necessary funds in case of medical emergency .
  • To extend helping Hands to needy families who lost their sole earning family member.
  • To Create a platform with easy technical accessibility for extending help to all.
  • To provide a quality health support system with advanced medical assistance.
  • To establish a transparent system for help where people can rely on without any doubt in their minds.
  • To bring advancement in the field of medicine by joint effort of medical institutions and government, non-government agencies.
  • To offer free medical checkups at regular intervals.
  • To provide affordable and effective medical solutions by combining Ayurveda and allopathic medical science.
  • To create a healthy planet.
