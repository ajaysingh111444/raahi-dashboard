What is our monthly subscription plan?

With our monthly subscription plan, you can contribute a small share from your income every month into our monthly fund collection. At the time of a medical emergency, this fund can be used to support families. By donating a little amount every month you can create a big difference in the lives of people.

What we do?

We are taking the following initiatives for your health:

  • We are creating a contingency fund which will be utilised to save lives of people who are suffering from a deadly medical condition and are unable to pay for treatment.
  • Creating easy accessibility to medical services through technical advancement.
  • Upgrading of the existing medical system and removing barriers in the healthcare system.
  • Creating a transparent system for raising funds for a medical emergency and an easy to reach platform for the needy.
  • Connecting medical researchers, government cells, a non-profit organisation, hospitals and other medical institutions under one umbrella to create a better and healthy society.
  • Join us to see healthy people creating a healthy planet.
