Let's Save Ajaz and His Family From The Wrath of Blood Cancer.
Ajaz is a 28 years old boy suffering from Blood Cancer and is undergoing treatment at MGM Medical Centre and Research Institute, Aurangabad. He resides in Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh, India and makes his living by running a small cloth shop. His earnings are too low to pay for his medical expenses as he manages day to day expenses with great difficulty.
The ongoing treatment of Ajaz has consumed all his family savings and has brought a big financial burden over them. Being the sole earning member of his family has placed their future in dark.
The treatment has already put them under a debt of 4,00,000/-
